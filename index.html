01 / Research

Underground Tunnel 3D Mapping

Northeastern University RTAB-Map SLAM ZED Mini · IMU

Built a 3D mapping system for Northeastern's 16,000-foot underground tunnel network — a GPS-denied environment with repetitive geometry, the kind of place that breaks most visual SLAM stacks. Combined RTAB-Map's loop-closure detection and memory management with a ZED Mini stereo camera and IMU, fusing the streams to hold accurate pose over long loops and generate high-resolution point clouds of the entire network.

02 / In progress

ROS2 SLAM Testing Platform

Personal project ROS2 Multi-sensor

A robotics platform purpose-built to benchmark SLAM algorithms against each other on identical hardware. The chassis carries LIDAR, IMU, wheel encoders, stereo cameras, and ultrasonics, so any algorithm under test sees the same ground truth. 08 / Hardware

Closed-Loop BLDC Motor Controller

RollNDrive · Custom PCB For MDR product line

A custom closed-loop BLDC motor controller for the MDR product, using encoder feedback for precise speed regulation. The interesting part is the interface: instead of forcing customers to learn PWM, RS232, or RS485, I designed a digital-pin control scheme — connect a pin to positive logic, hit the speed listed on the chart. No protocol, no firmware on the customer side. Full safety suite: overvoltage, overcurrent, overtemperature, reverse voltage, and isolated inputs.

10 / Hardware

High-Power Brushed DC Motor Controller

Custom PCB MOSFET-based

A high-current motor controller for brushed DC motors — MOSFETs handle speed control and dedicated relays handle braking. The whole board is designed around heat: thermal mass for the MOSFET bank, active cooling, and protection layers (overcurrent, overvoltage, thermal cutoff) so the board fails safe under sustained heavy load.

17 / Agritech

Self-Propelled Onion Harvester

Team project · Electronics lead IC engine · Hydraulics

On a team that built a self-propelled IC-engine onion harvester, I owned the electronics: a closed-loop control system regulating the depth of the digging mechanism. A linear potentiometer measured depth, a solenoid valve drove the hydraulic cylinder, and the controller held the target depth across uneven field conditions. A load cell in the holding area logged harvest quantity in real time.
